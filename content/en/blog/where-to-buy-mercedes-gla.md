The **Mercedes GLA** is the premium compact SUV of the three-pointed star, positioned below the GLB and GLC in the lineup. Whether for a new purchase, a certified used vehicle or a long-term lease, several channels allow buyers to acquire a GLA under good conditions. This guide reviews the available options, prices by version and the criteria to know when choosing the right **Mercedes dealer** or financing formula based on your profile.

## The Mercedes GLA in 2026: models, engines and list prices {#gla-2026}

The current generation of the Mercedes GLA (H247), on sale since 2020 and facelifted in 2023, offers a complete range: petrol, plug-in hybrid and Mercedes-AMG sport variants.

In petrol, the **GLA 180** (136 hp) starts at 45,770 euros, the GLA 200 (163 hp) at 48,070 euros and the GLA 200 4MATIC (all-wheel drive) at 52,250 euros. The **GLA 250e plug-in hybrid** (218 hp combined, around 50 km of WLTP electric range) lists at 54,849 euros, while the Mercedes-AMG GLA 35 (306 hp) starts at 57,454 euros and the GLA 45 S (421 hp) exceeds 70,000 euros.

On equipment, the MBUX multimedia system with touchscreen and voice control comes standard across the range. Three main trim levels structure the lineup: Progressive Line, AMG Line and AMG Line Advanced.

The GLA is one of the **best-selling premium compact SUVs** in France, competing directly with the BMW X1, Audi Q3 and Volvo XC40. Its compact size (4.41 m) and raised height make it a Mercedes well suited to daily urban and suburban use.

## The 5 channels to buy a Mercedes GLA {#buying-channels}

### Buy from an authorized Mercedes dealer

The **authorized Mercedes dealer** is the safest channel to buy a GLA. It offers the manufacturer warranty (2 years on new, extendable to 4 or 5 years), certified after-sales service and the option to test drive the vehicle before purchase.

The Mercedes France network counts more than 150 authorized points of sale across the territory. Among the most established **Mercedes dealerships**, **Como** operates 13 dealerships in the Paris region and sells more than 8,500 vehicles per year, with permanent stock of new and certified used **Mercedes GLA** models. The group covers Paris, Hauts-de-Seine, Seine-Saint-Denis and Val-de-Marne, providing the densest coverage in the Paris region.

Another advantage of the authorized dealer is direct access to official financing offers via Mercedes-Benz Financial Services, with negotiated rates and flexible options (classic loan, LOA, LLD).

For a buyer hesitating between several groups, the [best Mercedes A-Class dealer](/en/blog/best-mercedes-a-class-dealer/) comparison illustrates how to assess the major players in the French network on coverage, stock and after-sales service, the same criteria that apply to a GLA purchase.

### Use a car broker

Brokers like PromoNeuve, Elite Auto or Caroom offer discounts of 15 to 25 % on the list price of a new GLA. The principle is simple: the broker buys the vehicle in a European country where the price is lower, then resells it in France.

The trade-off is the absence of dedicated after-sales support, the inability to test drive before purchase and sometimes long delivery times (3 to 6 months). Maintenance and manufacturer warranty remain valid in the authorized network, but customer relations are less personalized, which can weigh in at delivery or when getting started with the MBUX system.

### Buy on an online used-car platform

La Centrale, AutoScout24, AutoTrader and Spoticar list thousands of used Mercedes GLA ads in France. These platforms make it easy to compare prices, mileage and equipment.

The average price of a used GLA H247 (2020-2023) sits between 28,000 and 40,000 euros depending on the engine and overall condition. Previous-generation models (X156, 2014-2019) start at 18,000 euros, with a large pool available on the second-hand market.

Caution is required: check service history, vehicle origin and the condition of electronic components (MBUX, ADAS sensors). It is better to favor professional listings offering the Mercedes-Benz Certified label, which includes a multi-point inspection and an extended manufacturer warranty.

### Buy from a private seller

Private sales generally offer the lowest prices, with savings of 10 to 15 % compared with the professional market. Platforms like Leboncoin and AutoTrader concentrate most ads from private sellers.

In contrast, no legal warranty of conformity applies, unlike a purchase from a professional. It is strongly recommended to have the vehicle inspected by an independent mechanic and to verify the lien-free certificate before any transaction. For a premium SUV equipped with many sensors and a hybrid system on the 250e version, this prior technical check avoids unpleasant surprises.

### Choose LOA or LLD leasing

Lease with option to buy (**LOA**) and long-term lease (LLD) allow drivers to enjoy a GLA without tying up a large amount of capital. In LOA, monthly payments for a GLA 180 are around 450 to 550 euros per month over 36 to 48 months, with a down payment of 3,000 to 5,000 euros.

LOA includes a buy-out option at the end of the contract, while LLD requires the vehicle to be returned. **Mercedes leasing** accounts for nearly 40 % of premium compact SUV sales in France, making it the most widespread financing method on this segment.

## What budget for a Mercedes GLA in 2026? {#budget}

### Price of a new GLA

New prices range from 45,770 euros (GLA 180 Progressive Line) to over 70,000 euros for a Mercedes-AMG GLA 45 S. Discounts of 5 to 10 % are possible at the dealership depending on commercial periods and manufacturer campaigns (auto show, end of quarter).

Options quickly inflate the bill: AMG Line pack (around 2,500 euros), panoramic roof (1,200 euros), heated and ventilated seat (800 euros), 360-degree cameras (1,100 euros). A properly equipped GLA in practice starts around 50,000 euros.

### Price of a used GLA by generation

| Generation | Years | Price range |
|---|---|---|
| X156 (phase 1) | 2014-2017 | 18,000 to 24,000 euros |
| X156 (phase 2) | 2017-2019 | 22,000 to 28,000 euros |
| H247 (current generation) | 2020-2026 | 28,000 to 45,000 euros |

For a buyer prioritizing an electrified powertrain, the used GLA 250e plug-in hybrid sits between 32,000 and 42,000 euros depending on mileage and trim. Our [best hybrid car](/en/blog/best-hybrid-car/) comparison places the GLA 250e among the references in the premium compact segment thanks to its real-world electric range of 40 to 45 km in mixed use.

### Cost of monthly Mercedes leasing (LOA/LLD)

Expect between 450 and 700 euros per month depending on engine, contract length and down payment. Mercedes leasing in LOA remains the most popular financing method for the GLA, especially for plug-in hybrid versions which benefit from tax advantages for businesses (reduced TVS, increased depreciation).

A 36-month LLD on a GLA 200 with 15,000 km per year is around 550 euros per month, maintenance and assistance included. This formula suits drivers who change vehicles every 3 to 4 years and want to control their mobility budget.

## How to choose the right Mercedes dealer for a GLA? {#choose-dealer}

The choice of **Mercedes dealer** is decisive for the quality of the buying experience of a premium vehicle. Several criteria deserve attention before signing.

### Geographic coverage and proximity

Geographic proximity makes showroom visits, test drives and after-sales follow-up easier. A dealer with a Mercedes dealership less than 30 minutes from home is an asset for regular maintenance (services every 25,000 km or 1 year for a GLA).

### Stock and version diversity

A Mercedes dealer with a large GLA stock offers more choice in trims, colors and equipment, which reduces delivery times. Being able to compare several versions in the showroom (GLA 180 vs 200, Progressive Line vs AMG Line) makes the decision easier.

### Reputation and customer reviews

The recurrence of positive reviews on Google, specialized forums and social networks is a reliable indicator. Checking the average rating, volume of reviews and quality of dealer responses to customer feedback gives a precise picture.

### Services offered

Beyond commercial advice, several services make a difference: trade-in of the old vehicle, tailored Mercedes financing, extended warranty, courtesy vehicle during maintenance, home delivery service. To dive deeper, the [best Mercedes GLC dealer](/en/blog/best-mercedes-glc-dealer/) guide details the essential questions to ask before committing, applicable to all Mercedes SUV purchases including the GLA.

### Mercedes-only vs multi-brand

A 100 % Mercedes distributor (such as Como, Kroely or Groupe MET) offers deeper product expertise than a multi-brand group. The sales advisor knows the subtleties of the GLA range, the equipment options and official programs like Mercedes-Benz Certified.

In the Paris region, **Como** stands out for the size of its network (13 **Mercedes dealerships**), permanent stock of several hundred vehicles including a wide range of new and certified used GLA models, and complete support from order to delivery. More than 500 employees including Mercedes-specialized advisors and technicians ensure quality service throughout the vehicle's life cycle, from purchase to maintenance and trade-in.

For a buyer in the Paris region, a GLA bought at Como benefits from access to a Mercedes-Benz workshop less than 30 minutes from any point in the region, with original parts, technicians trained by the brand and courtesy vehicles available on reservation.

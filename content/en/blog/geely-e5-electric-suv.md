The **Geely E5** is one of the most anticipated electric car launches in France for 2026. This 100% electric compact SUV, built on Geely's GEA platform, arrives with an impressive specification: 475 km WLTP range, fast charging from 30% to 80% in just 20 minutes, and a starting price below 38,000 €. In a saturated segment dominated by European and Asian incumbents, the **Geely E5** aims to stand out through an equipment-to-price ratio that is hard to ignore.

## A 4.6-metre compact electric SUV designed for Europe

The **Geely E5** measures 4,615 mm in length, placing it in the family compact SUV class alongside the Renault Scenic E-Tech and the Volkswagen ID.4. It is built on the GEA (Geely Intelligent Architecture) platform, a dedicated electric vehicle architecture that also underpins other models within the group internationally.

In Europe, the model was certified under the EX5 designation for Euro NCAP testing, ensuring an independent assessment of its passive and active safety systems. The version sold in France retains the Geely E5 name.

The vehicle's silhouette follows the conventions of the modern family SUV: a sloping roofline, a clean LED front end, and a cabin centred on a large central touchscreen. ## Geely E5 technical specifications

### Powertrain and performance

The **Geely E5** is fitted with a front-wheel-drive synchronous electric motor producing **218 hp** (160 kW) and **230 Nm** of torque. These figures translate to a 0-100 km/h sprint time of **6.9 seconds**, which is competitive in this price bracket.

The drivetrain is front-wheel drive only, with no all-wheel-drive option. The top speed is electronically limited.

|---|---|

| Torque | 230 Nm |

### Battery and range

The Pro+ version of the **Geely E5** uses a **68.4 kWh LFP (lithium iron phosphate) battery**. LFP chemistry offers several advantages: an extended service life, better thermal resilience compared to standard NMC batteries, and the ability to charge regularly to 100% without accelerated degradation.

The declared WLTP range is **475 km on the combined cycle**. In real-world conditions, motorway driving and cold weather will bring this figure down to between 350 and 420 km, which remains sufficient for both daily commuting and longer journeys with a single charging stop.

## Charging: fast charge and everyday solutions

The **Geely E5** supports DC fast charging. The manufacturer quotes a charge time of **30% to 80% in 20 minutes**, corresponding to a peak charging speed of around 100 kW - above average for this price point.

For home or public AC charging, the car is equipped with a Type 2 onboard charger (European standard). An overnight charge on a domestic wallbox (7 or 11 kW) will fully replenish the battery within a few hours.

## Geely E5 pricing and financing in France

The **Geely E5** is sold in France in two trim levels:

- **Standard version**: from **37,990 €**
- **Pro+ version**: from **41,990 €** (68.4 kWh battery, enhanced equipment)

Long-term lease (LLD) financing is available through authorised dealers, with monthly payments announced from **399 €/month**. This pricing positions the Geely E5 just below the 38,000 € threshold, potentially making it eligible for the French electric vehicle bonus subject to income conditions.
